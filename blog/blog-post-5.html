# What is 33% of 300? A Quick and Easy Guide

Are you trying to figure out what 33% of 300 is? You've landed in the right place! It's a common question, and understanding how to solve it can make everything from calculating discounts to understanding statistics a lot easier.

The short and simple answer is: 33% of 300 is 99.

But how do we arrive at that number? Let's break down the process. There are a couple of very simple ways to think about it.

Breaking Down the Calculation

Knowing the "why" behind the math can be incredibly helpful. Here are two straightforward methods to find the answer.

Method 1: The Decimal Method

This is the most common way to solve percentage problems and is super reliable for any numbers you might face.

  • Step 1: Convert the percentage into a decimal. The word "percent" literally means "per 100," so 33% is the same as 33 out of 100, which can be written as the decimal 0.33.
  • Step 2: Multiply the decimal by the total number. In this case, you'll multiply 0.33 by 300.
  • Result: 0.33 x 300 = 99.

Method 2: The "Per Hundred" Common Sense Trick

This method can be more intuitive and works great for numbers that are multiples of 100, like 300.

  • Step 1: Understand what 33% means. As we said, it means "33 for every 100."
  • Step 2: Look at your total number. Our number is 300. How many "100s" are in 300? There are three! (100 + 100 + 100).
  • Step 3: Put it all together. If you get 33 for each of those three "100s," you simply add them up: 33 + 33 + 33 = 99.

Both methods get you to the same correct answer: 99.
