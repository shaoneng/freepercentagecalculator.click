What is 17 Percent of 80? A Simple Step-by-Step Guide

Ever found yourself staring at a price tag with a weird discount, or trying to solve a homework problem, and you're faced with a question like "what is 17 percent of 80?" You're not alone! Percentages are a part of daily life, and knowing how to calculate them is a fantastic skill to have. Don't worry, it's easier than it looks.

Let's get straight to the point. The quick answer is:

17% of 80 is 13.6.

But how do we get that answer? Let's break it down so you can solve any percentage problem you come across.

How to Calculate 17% of 80

The easiest way to find the percentage of a number is to convert the percentage into a decimal and then multiply. It's just two simple steps.

Step 1: Convert the Percentage to a Decimal

The word "percent" literally means "per hundred." So, to convert a percentage to a decimal, you just need to divide it by 100. A simple trick is to move the decimal point two places to the left.

For our problem, 17% becomes:

17 / 100 = 0.17

Step 2: Multiply the Decimal by the Original Number

Now that you have your decimal, all you need to do is multiply it by the number you want to find the percentage of, which in this case is 80.

0.17 * 80 = 13.6

And there you have it! You've successfully calculated that 17% of 80 is 13.6.

A Quick Way to Check Your Answer

Does the answer make sense? Let's do a quick mental check:

  • We know that 10% of 80 is 8 (just move the decimal one place to the left).
  • We also know that 20% of 80 would be double that, which is 16.

Since 17% is between 10% and 20%, our answer should be between 8 and 16. Our result, 13.6, fits perfectly in that range!
