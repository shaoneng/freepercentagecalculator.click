# What is 10% off 180? A Quick and Easy Guide

Whether you're eyeing a new pair of shoes, figuring out a service fee, or just doing some quick math, you've hit the right question: what is 10% off of 180? It's a common calculation, and understanding how to do it quickly can be a real-life superpower.

Let's get straight to the point.

The short answer is: 10% of 180 is 18. So, if you take 10% off 180, you are left with 162.

But how did we get that answer? And how can you calculate it for yourself next time? Let's break it down in a way that's super easy to remember.

How to Calculate 10% off 180

There are a couple of simple ways to figure this out. We'll start with the quickest mental math trick!

The "Move the Decimal" Trick (The Easiest Way)

Calculating 10% of any number is surprisingly simple. All you have to do is take the original number (180) and move the decimal point one place to the left.

  • Original number: 180.0
  • Move the decimal one spot to the left: 18.00

And just like that, you have your answer! The discount is $18.

To find the final price, you just subtract this discount from the original number:

180 - 18 = 162

The Universal Percentage Formula

  3. Subtract the discount from the original price: Finally, 180 - 18 = 162. This is your final price.

Quick Reference: Other Discounts on 180

Now that you know the method, let's see how it applies to other common discounts:

  • 5% off 180: Half of 10%, so half of 18 is 9. Final price: 171.
  • 15% off 180: That's 10% + 5%. So, 18 + 9 = 27. Final price: 153.
  • 20% off 180: Double 10%, so double 18 is 36. Final price: 144.
  • 25% off 180: A quarter of 180. 180 / 4 = 45. Final price: 135.
